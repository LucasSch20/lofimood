   Welcome to lofimood! a plataform where you can listen to lofi musics whenever you want, while you study, work, workout, or do whatever you are doing!

   The gifs on this site are dinamic, in other words, they will adjust in order to your sistem timezone, changing from day to night, in order to you have a better experience while listening to lofi hip hop beats.

   Lofimood is still on alpha version, so it's normal to appear some bugs and some features dont work properly. If it happens, i would be glad if you send me an email through the first header icon.

   Oh! Just to remember, this site use cookies to make your experience better, if you accept, click on the button bellow.
